#  Tax Rules

##  Objective
To calculate monthly TDS based on annual income.

---

##  Tax Slabs (Old Regime)

| Income Range       | Tax Rate |
|------------------|----------|
| 0 - 2.5L         | 0%       |
| 2.5L - 5L        | 5%       |
| 5L - 10L         | 20%      |
| Above 10L        | 30%      |

---

##  Calculation Logic

1. Convert monthly salary → annual income
2. Apply slab-based taxation
3. Divide by 12 for monthly deduction

---

##  Example

Annual Income: ₹8,00,000

Tax:
- 2.5L → 0
- 2.5L → 5%
- 3L → 20%

Total = ₹1,12,500

Monthly TDS = ₹9,375
